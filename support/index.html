General Questions

What is ChatrBox, and how does it work?
ChatrBox is a smart pet training device that detects motion and plays alerts or voice messages to correct behavior.
What are the main features of ChatrBox?
Motion-activated playback, LED lights, Wi-Fi support, and custom voice recordings.
How can ChatrBox help with pet training?
It reinforces good behavior by triggering responses when your pet acts appropriately or inappropriately.
What is included in the ChatrBox package?
Device, 5 focus shields, 4 AAA batteries, USB cable, and guide.
What types of pets can use ChatrBox?
Dogs, cats, and other trainable pets.
Does it require internet?
No, unless using the Wi-Fi training mode.
Can I use it outdoors?
Indoors recommended; outdoor use must be dry and shaded.
Does it work for both dogs and cats?
Yes, with customizable messaging for either species.

Setup & Installation

How do I set up ChatrBox?
Install batteries, power on, record a message, place in desired location.
What is the Wi-Fi for?
Wi-Fi allows manual triggering during observed training via smartphone.
How do I enter Wi-Fi mode?
Hold the volume button for 3 seconds, then connect your phone to the device Wi-Fi.
How do I disconnect Wi-Fi?
Click END on the training page. Your phone will revert to its normal network.
Can it work without Wi-Fi?
Yes, automatic motion detection and alerts work without internet.
