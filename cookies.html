What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device to help the website function properly, improve user experience, and gather analytics data.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for basic website functionality, such as navigation and access to secure areas.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our website by collecting anonymous information.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and enhance your experience on our site.

Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to manage cookie preferences, delete cookies, or block certain types of cookies entirely.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services like analytics tools or embedded content (e.g., videos) that may set their own cookies. These cookies are governed by the privacy policies of those third parties.
