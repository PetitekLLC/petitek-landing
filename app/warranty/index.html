What’s Covered

If your ChatrBox device experiences a manufacturing defect during the warranty period, Petitek will, at its discretion:

This warranty applies only to products purchased through authorized channels, including Amazon.com.

What’s Not Covered

  • Loss or theft of the product

How to Make a Warranty Claim

To initiate a claim, contact our support team at:
support@petitek.com
