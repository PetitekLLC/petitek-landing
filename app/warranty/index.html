Warranty Information

Thank you for choosing ChatrBox! We stand behind the quality of our products and offer a 90-day limited warranty from the date of purchase.

What's Covered

This warranty covers any manufacturing defects or malfunctions under normal use during the warranty period. If a defect arises during the warranty period, we will repair or replace the product at no additional cost.

What's Not Covered

  • Damage caused by misuse, abuse, or accident
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Modifications or unauthorized repairs
  • Damage due to water, impact, or extreme conditions

How to Make a Warranty Claim

To initiate a warranty claim, please contact us at support@petitek.com with the following:

  • Proof of purchase
  • Product serial number
  • Brief description of the issue

Once verified, we will provide instructions for repair or replacement.

Need Help?
