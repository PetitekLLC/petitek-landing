Why the first weeks matter

New environments bring new routines, boundaries, and expectations. Adjustment is normal — and early support can make the transition easier for both pets and the people who care for them.

Every home is new. Calm support helps pets adjust.

Support makes the difference

With time, consistency, and the right support, pets begin to feel safe and at ease in their new homes.
